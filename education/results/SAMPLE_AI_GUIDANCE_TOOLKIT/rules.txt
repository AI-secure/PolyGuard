1. Using AI tools to manipulate media to impersonate others for bullying, harassment, or any form of intimidation is strictly prohibited.
2. Copying from any source, including generative AI, without prior approval and adequate documentation is prohibited.
3. Submitting AI-generated work as original work by students is prohibited.
4. Using AI technologies to supplant the role of human educators in instructing and nurturing students is prohibited.
5. Using AI tools to monitor classrooms for accountability purposes, such as analyzing teacher-student interactions or tracking teacher movements, is prohibited.
6. Using AI in ways that compromise teacher or student privacy or lead to unauthorized data collection is prohibited.
7. Entering confidential or personally identifiable information into unauthorized AI tools is prohibited.
