Risk Category 1: Harassment & Discrimination
1. Do not engage in any form of workplace harassment.  
2. Do not discriminate on the basis of age, color, race, religion, sex (including pregnancy), sexual orientation, gender identity or expression, national origin, disability, veteran status, or any other characteristic protected by law.

Risk Category 2: Workplace Violence & Threats
3. Do not commit or threaten any act of violence in the workplace.

Risk Category 3: Substance Abuse & Fitness for Duty
4. Do not work while under the influence of alcohol, illegal drugs, or misused prescription medication.

Risk Category 4: Investigation Integrity & Information Management
5. Do not conceal, falsify, or destroy information relevant to an internal investigation.
